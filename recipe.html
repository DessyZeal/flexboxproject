Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preperation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 mins
  • Preperation:5 mins
  • Cooking:5 mins

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • Optional filling: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs.

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of milk for a fluffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of milk for a fluffier texture.
  3. Cook the omelette: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of milk for a fluffier texture.
  4. Add fillings(Optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly running in the middle, sprinkle your choosen filling over one half of your omelette.
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it unto a plate.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
